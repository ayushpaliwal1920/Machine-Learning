##### ASSOCIATION RULE LEARNING ######

Association rule learning is a machine learning technique used to discover interesting relationships or pattern in large datasets,

It is often applied to transactional data , where items are bought or used together.

a rule-based machine learning method used to discover interesting, hidden relationships (rules) between variables in large datasets. 
It identifies patterns in the form "if-then" (), commonly used in market basket analysis to determine items frequently bought together.
 Key metrics to evaluate these rules include Support, Confidence, and Lift


Association rule learning work : 

1. Support  , 

2. Confidence , 

3. Lift
